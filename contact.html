Common questions

Before you book.

Quick answers to what most patients ask before their first visit.

Are consultations really free?
Yes. Our doctors meet you, assess your goals, and give you an honest plan with no pressure to book a treatment that day. We only charge if you proceed with a clinical procedure.
Do I need health insurance to be seen?
No. Aesthetic and most cosmetic dental treatments are self-pay. For medical dental work, we accept most major UAE insurance providers. Bring your card and we'll verify coverage at reception.
Are your doctors DHA licensed?
Every clinician at Silk holds a current Dubai Health Authority license. The clinic itself is licensed and inspected by the DHA, which is required for any provider operating inside Dubai Healthcare City.
What languages do you speak?
English and Arabic are spoken by every doctor. We also have French, Hindi, Urdu, and Russian speaking team members on rotation. Tell us your preference when booking.
Can I see before and after photos?
Yes, in clinic. UAE regulations restrict where we can post patient before/after images publicly, so we share these in person during your consultation with the patient's written consent on file.
What's your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 4 hours before your appointment via WhatsApp. We don't charge no-show fees on consultations. For booked treatments, a 50% deposit may apply on rescheduling within 24 hours.
