Botox in Dubai.

Precision neuromodulator injections for fine lines, wrinkles and expression softening. Doctor-led, conservative, and tailored to your facial anatomy.

What it is

Why choose Botox at Silk.

Botox remains the most researched aesthetic treatment in the world, but the difference between a natural result and an overdone one is entirely in the hands of the injector. At Silk, every Botox patient sees a specialist doctor for the consultation and the treatment itself. We map your muscle activity on the first visit, document dosing, and compare photographs at every follow-up.

The goal is always the same: softer expressions, not frozen ones.

Benefits

What you can expect.

  • Smoothing of forehead lines, crow's feet and frown lines
  • Softer jawline via masseter botox (if clinically indicated)
  • Treatment of excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis)
  • Migraine relief when medically appropriate
  • Results visible within 3 to 7 days, peak at 2 weeks
  • Effects typically last 3 to 4 months
Process

What a Botox visit looks like.

  1. Consultation. We study your facial anatomy, muscle strength and goals. No pressure to proceed.
  2. Mapping. The doctor marks injection points and confirms units with you.
  3. Injection. Precise injections with ultrafine needles. Total time 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Aftercare brief. Clear instructions printed and sent by WhatsApp.
  5. Follow-up. A complimentary review at 2 weeks to check symmetry and adjust if needed.
