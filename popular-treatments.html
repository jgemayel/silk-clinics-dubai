Scarlet S

Radio-frequency microneedling for natural lift and tightening.

Scarlet S delivers short-pulse radiofrequency energy through ultrafine needles, stimulating collagen and elastin deep in the dermis. Results build over two to three months, without altering your facial structure.

  • Best for skin laxity, jawline definition, acne scars
  • Session time 45 minutes
  • Downtime 24 to 48 hours
  • Protocol 3 sessions, 4 weeks apart
